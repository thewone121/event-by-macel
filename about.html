About Event by Macel

I.Our Story

Built from Dansoman, one celebration at a time

Event by Macel began in Dansoman, Accra, out of a simple belief: that a beautifully planned occasion shouldn't be reserved for the few who can afford to leave every detail to chance. Our founder, Macel, started by planning celebrations for friends and family — and quickly discovered a gift for turning modest budgets into unforgettable moments.

Since 2018, that early reputation has grown into a full-service planning and styling studio trusted by couples, families and corporate clients across Accra and beyond — without ever losing the personal attention that defined our very first events.

Macel Andoh

Founder & Lead Planner
II.What Guides Us

Mission, vision & philosophy

Our Mission

To help clients create special moments that suit both their personal style and their budget — with no detail overlooked and no stress carried alone.

Our Vision

To be West Africa's most trusted name in bespoke event planning — known as much for our integrity with vendors and budgets as for our design.

Our Planning Philosophy

Good planning is invisible. Our job is to carry every logistic, deadline and vendor conversation so that you experience only the finished moment.
